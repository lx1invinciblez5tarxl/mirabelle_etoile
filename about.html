The Cute Little Artist

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio site!

I'm just another aspiring digital and graphic artist, looking for some exciting artwork to give to someone to cherish! Sorry for the big block of text! Skip it if it's a bore, but take the time to look further down this page for a better idea of my skills.

I studied under the game design major and visual arts minor to explore my interests in everything related to art. Game art, fine arts, digital arts, 2D and 3D animation, graphic and web design, I've dabbled in them all. I've worked solo in projects and collaborated with classmates in others. After all those years in college, I've settled primarily on honing my digital and graphic art, though may consider making 2D game assets in the future.

While my main art style is cute, I have done other projects of various themes as well. I'm happy to try other styles to the best of my ability. I hope to expand my skills further!

Skills

  • Experienced with using and adapting to multiple software programs
  • Apt in graphic art and design, such as for digital images, web icons/buttons, and prints
  • Adept at creating pixel art and animation
  • Moderately skilled at digital painting and animation
  • Able to construct simple, user-friendly static websites in HTML and CSS
  • Basic knowledge of photography techniques and photo editing, including shooting and editing of RAW images.

Software Experience (As of 3/13/19)

Aseprite : My primary program for pixel art and animation. They can be for digital display, prints, and even game assets.

Brackets : My main web design tool for HTML and CSS. Used to develop this site.

Clip Studio Paint : My primary application and alternative to Photoshop for digital painting.

Inkscape : Alternative to Illustrator for vector art. Currently learning to use.

GIMP : Alternative to Photoshop for photo editing. Currently learning to use.

dark table : Alternative to Camera Raw for editing RAW image files. Currently learning to use.

Adobe Illustrator : For vector art, some of which are applicable to web icons and logos as demonstrated by this portfolio site.

Adobe Photoshop : For digital painting, graphic design, photo editing, and some animation. 2D game assets were made in this as well as prints.

Adobe InDesign : For graphic design of prints, such as paper books and posters.

Adobe Camera Raw : Used to edit photos taken as RAW image files.
